# Customer Analysis

## Population Overview
The location at Nanjing West Road, specifically around the Jing'an Kerry Centre, presents a strong consumer base for the prospective coffee shop. The population densities at various radii are significant, illustrating a dense urban environment conducive to retail and service establishments:

- **300m radius**: Approximately 4,936 people reside within this close radius, indicating a highly vibrant and populated local area.
- **500m radius**: The population expands to 13,274 individuals, showcasing a considerable increase, suggesting robust potential foot traffic.
- **1000m radius**: A substantial population of approximately 68,254 people, which includes a diverse and wide-ranging demographic, further supports the area's appeal.
- **1500m radius**: The population spikes to 176,256, emphasizing the area's role as a central hub within the city, attracting a diverse group of city dwellers.

## Age Demographics
The age distribution within these radii aligns well with the target customer base of young professionals and students, a prime market for boutique coffee shops:

- **300m radius**: The majority of the population (2,893) is between ages 15-59, typically representing young professionals and likely customers for the boutique coffee shop model.
- **500m radius**: Continued dominance of the age group 15-59 with 7,716 individuals suggests an abundant presence of working-age individuals.
- **1000m radius**: There are 39,745 individuals in the 15-59 age range, reinforcing strong daytime traffic potential from office workers and students.
- **1500m radius**: As expected in this bustling metropolitan area, the 15-59 age group comprises 103,067 individuals. This critical mass strongly supports the coffee shop's targeted demographic.

## Customer Potential Assessment
The significant number of young professionals within proximity suggests excellent potential for customer acquisition, especially considering the desirable pricing and service model:

1. **Strong morning traffic**: The area is populated with working individuals likely seeking convenient and quality coffee options on their way to work.
2. **Evening social gatherings**: The neighborhood's population density supports steady customer traffic throughout the day into the evening, aligning with leisure and social activities.
3. **Moderate pricing**: The price level is appropriate for this socioeconomic demographic, sustaining both frequent and spontaneous visits.

## Data Quality & Limitations
Data derived from WorldPop indicates accurate estimations typically used for urban studies, with a careful note on potential fluctuations in demographics not captured in these estimates, especially seasonal or emergent changes (e.g., events, festivals). The absence of granular age-related activities (such as university campuses or large office spaces) may require supplementary qualitative insight for precise targeting strategies.

Overall, the densely populated and demographically suitable location holds strong potential for establishing and growing a highly successful boutique coffee shop.
