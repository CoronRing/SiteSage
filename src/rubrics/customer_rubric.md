# **Customer Potential Rubric (Location-Oriented)**
## **Evaluation Criteria**
Score the *location’s customer potential* on a scale of **0–10**, based on the strength of its customer base and its alignment with the target store type.

## **1. Population Density & Catchment Strength (30%)**
### Excellent (9–10)

* High residential or worker population at multiple radii
* Strong catchment area clearly supporting retail
* Population distribution matches typical retail thresholds

### Good (7–8)

* Solid population density within reasonable distance

### Adequate (5–6)

* Moderate population; some ability to support retail

### Poor (3–4)

* Low nearby population; weak catchment zone

### Insufficient (0–2)

* Very sparse population; unlikely to sustain a store

---

## **2. Demographic Fit for Target Store Type (35%)**

### Excellent (9–10)

* Strong alignment of age, income, and lifestyle with target customers
* Clear purchasing power and relevance to store concept

### Good (7–8)

* Mostly aligned with target customers

### Adequate (5-6)

* Some alignment; mixed suitability

### Poor (3-4)

* Weak demographic match

### Insufficient (1-2)

* Demographics do not support the store type

---

## **3. Customer Behavior & Demand Potential (20%)**

### Excellent

* Clear indicators of recurring demand (residential stability, commuters, workplace clusters)
* Temporal patterns (morning/evening) support store business model

### Good

* Stable or predictable demand patterns

### Adequate

* Some demand signals; uncertain strength

### Poor

* Weak demand potential

### Insufficient

* No meaningful signs of customer demand

---

## **4. Opportunities & Risks (15%)**

### Excellent

* Demonstrated growth potential or favorable demographic trends

### Good

* Some opportunities; manageable risks

### Adequate

* Neutral risk/opportunity profile

### Poor

* Noticeable risks limiting customer potential

### Insufficient

* High-risk customer environment

---

## **Output Format**

```json
{
  "score": 8.2,
  "justification": "The location offers strong population density and excellent alignment with young professionals, indicating clear customer potential. Demand patterns suggest consistent foot traffic from residents and office workers, with minimal downside risk."
}
```
