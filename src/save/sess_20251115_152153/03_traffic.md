### Accessibility and Traffic Report for Boutique Coffee Shop

Located at 南京东路300号, 黄浦区, 上海, the Boutique Coffee Shop benefits from:

- **Proximity to Transit:**
  - The nearest transit point, a bus stop, is just 120 meters away.
  - Another bus station and a further bus stop are available nearby.

- **Parking Facilities:**
  - There are 30 parking lots in the vicinity, ensuring ample parking options.

The high density of transport and parking options indicates that the store is easily accessible, making it convenient for both patrons and suppliers.
