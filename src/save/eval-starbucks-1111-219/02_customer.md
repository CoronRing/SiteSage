  - Morning commuter base in the **tens of thousands**.

- **Dense office employment cluster**
  - Within 600 m, likely **40,000–60,000 office workers**, underpinning repeat morning and midday coffee demand.

  - **~19k residents within 500 m**, **~86k within 1 km**, providing local off‑peak demand.

  - Many individuals are counted in multiple roles (commuter + office worker + shopper), leading to **habit formation** and repeat purchases.

### Cons / Risks

- **Intense competition**
  - Xujiahui core already has **multiple domestic and international coffee chains** (e.g., Starbucks, Luckin, Manner, etc.). Differentiation in taste, speed, and price is necessary to achieve upper‑range conversions.

- **High rental and labor cost**
  - Being inside 美罗城 in the Xujiahui hub implies **high fixed costs**, which require you to reach **mid‑ to high‑range daily volumes** to sustain profitability.

- **Peak‑time congestion**
  - Morning peak congestion and queue aversion may **constrain practical conversion rates** even if foot traffic is high. Operational efficiency (order‑ahead, pickup shelves, multiple espresso stations) is critical.

- **Sensitive to macro & mall performance**
  - If Metro City’s overall traffic declines due to competition or economic changes, your accessible customer pool may shrink.
