# Site Selection Report for Boutique Coffee Shop

## Executive Summary
The proposed location for the Boutique Coffee Shop at 上海市普陀区武宁路 achieved a final score of 4.78. Despite the competitive landscape, the location's excellent transit accessibility and reasonable customer potential make it a viable option, provided the business can differentiate itself effectively in the market.

## Site Overview
**Address:** 上海市普陀区武宁路.
Located in a thriving area of Shanghai, 武宁路 provides a bustling urban setting with significant pedestrian traffic due to numerous transit points nearby.

## Customer Insights
- **Total Population in 300m Radius:** Approximately 9,866.
- **Age Distribution:**
  - 0-14 years: 762
  - 15-59 years: 5,573
  - 60-64 years: 995
  - 65+ years: 2,274

The population distribution suggests a focus on working professionals and an aging population, aligning with business goals to cater to both commuters and local residents.

## Traffic & Accessibility
- **Transit Proximity:** 6 subway and metro stations, 1 bus station within a short walk.
- **Nearest Transit:** Only 13 meters away, enhancing ease of accessibility for customers.
- **Parking Facilities:** 30 parking spots available, facilitating car user access.

## Competition Landscape
- **Density of Competitors:**
  - 500m radius: 8 competitors
  - 1km radius: 10 competitors
  - 1.5km radius: 32 competitors
- **Nearest Competitor:** Located just 13 meters away.

This high density indicates a mature coffee market, requiring distinct branding and service offerings to capture market share.

## Weighting Rationale
- **Customer (20%):** While relatively smaller, the base remains crucial for local occupancy.
- **Traffic (40%):** High weight due to proximity to transit adding potential captive foot traffic.
- **Competition (40%):** Adoption of strategies to face high competitive pressure is essential.

## Recommendation
- **Actionable Steps:**
  - Develop unique value propositions to distinguish from nearby competitors.
  - Leverage transit access for promotional outreach (e.g., commuter specials).
  - Consider partnerships or community events to draw traffic.
- **Risks:** High competition may limit immediate penetration unless effectively countered by marketing and unique offerings.
