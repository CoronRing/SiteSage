- A highly tailored customer experience focusing on the cozy, boutique vibe promoted in the business description—emphasizing ambience, seating design, and perhaps partnerships with local artists to create a distinctive café experience.

- **Customer Base Support**: The substantial population and pre-aligned demographics support the entry of an additional coffee shop, provided differentiation strategies are robustly implemented.
